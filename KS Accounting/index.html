K.S ACCOUNTANCY SOLUTIONS

Accountants Enthusiastic About Numbers

Accountants based in Kidderminster providing proactive recommendations and working together with you to assist with your accountancy and taxation requirements.

Paul Concannon setup K.S Accountancy Solutions in 2013 having accumulated a wealth of experience in dealing with owner-managed businesses. He is enthusiastic about sharing the vast amount of knowledge and experience that he and his company have gained with their clients, and assist with their business and financial requirements any which way they can.

What we do...

ACCOUNTANCY
  • Year-end accounts preparation (Sole trader; Partnership; Limited Company; LLP)
  • Periodic Management Accounts preparation
  • Business startup advice and support
  • Filing of financial statements at Companies House and HMRC
TAXATION
  • Personal (Self-Assessment) and Business Taxation
  • Tax Planning
  • Assistance with in dealing with all other taxes (including VAT; PAYE; Capital Gains Tax; Inheritance Tax)
  • Taking care of your HMRC filing obligations
BOOKKEEPING & PAYROLL
  • Maintaining accounting records
  • Payroll management and assistance - including RTI compliance & Auto Enrolment (Pension)
  • CIS (Construction Industry Scheme) management and assistance
  • Advice and/or preparation of your VAT Return
SECRETARIAL SERVICES
  • Company Formations
  • Annual Return completion & submission
  • Statutory information updates and amendments
  • Preparation of statutory documents (e.g. Board Minutes and Dividend Vouchers)
